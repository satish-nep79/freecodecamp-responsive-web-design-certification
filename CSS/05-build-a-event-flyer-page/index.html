Tihar Celebration 2026: The Festival of Lights

Experience the Magic of Tihar

Join us for an unforgettable evening as we bring the warmth and brilliance of Nepal's beloved festival to our community. Tihar is a time of gratitude, illumination, and joy. Come witness the venue transform into a sea of glowing diyas (oil lamps) and vibrant marigold decorations.

Feature List

  • Deusi-Bhailo Performances:

    Enjoy traditional folk music and dancing that captures the true spirit of the festival.

  • Rangoli Competition:

    Participate in creating intricate, colorful sand art patterns at the entrance to welcome prosperity.

  • Traditional Feasts:

    Savor authentic festive delicacies, including Selroti (sweet rice donuts), sweets, and hot spiced tea.

Honor the Traditions

Tihar is unique because it dedicates different days to honoring animals and sacred bonds. While our main event condenses these beautiful traditions into one spectacular evening, we will be highlighting the core cultural rituals that make this festival so meaningful.

Event Timeline

  • 🪔 5:00 PM – Lighting of the Diyas:

    Help us illuminate the entire venue to symbolize the victory of light over darkness.

  • 🎭 6:30 PM – Cultural Showcase:

    Deep dive into the stories of Kaag Tihar (crow), Kukur Tihar (dog), and Bhai Tika (siblings).

  • 🍽️ 8:00 PM – Community Feast & Dance:

    Conclude the night with a shared dinner and open-floor dancing to traditional Tihar beats.
