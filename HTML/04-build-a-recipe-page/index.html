Easy Banana Pancakes

These fluffy, naturally sweet banana pancakes are incredibly easy to make and require just three simple ingredients. They are naturally gluten-free, packed with protein, and perfect for a quick, healthy breakfast that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, mash the ripe bananas thoroughly with a fork until mostly smooth.
  2. Whisk the eggs into the mashed bananas until the mixture is completely combined.
  3. Stir in the little flour and cinnamon until a consistent pancake batter forms, but don't make it runny
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat, grease lightly, and pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake, cooking for 2–3 minutes per side until golden brown.
